Welcome to Scrollie

What is Scrollie?

Scrollie is a randomizing featured content ad. It allows for two types of refeshing what content is showing

  1. Refresh the page, then a different content ad will show at the top of the site.
  2. The ad will also update if the user scroll out of the view point of the ad, then scrolls back to the top. The ad will refresh.

I tried to make sure that it was easy for the end user to add to their website.

Copy the script to your site and see the magic happen.
